AI PING PONG GAME







Instructions

  1. A point is won by a player when the opponent cannot hit the ball with a racket over the net and onto the other side of the table.
  2. A game is won by being the first player to win 21 points, and be at least 2 points ahead of his or her opponent.
  3. If both players have won 20 points, then the first player to get a 2 point lead wins the game
